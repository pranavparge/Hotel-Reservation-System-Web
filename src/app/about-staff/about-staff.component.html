About the Hotel Reservation System

As a staff member, logging into the Hotel Reservation System provides you with a versatile platform to efficiently handle all aspects of hotel operations. With your staff ID and password, you gain access to an intuitive dashboard and a feature-rich sidebar, enabling you to:

  • Comprehensive Booking Management: View all bookings in a centralized dashboard, organized for easy access and decision-making.
  • Room and Booking Operations: Create new rooms, update room statuses, and modify bookings as needed to accommodate customer requests or operational changes.
  • Streamlined Workflow: Quickly update booking details, cancel bookings, or reassign rooms to ensure seamless hotel operations.

Empowering you to deliver exceptional hospitality experiences every step of the way!
